Why This Matters

Phishing and online scams target everyone. Education protects people, reduces inequality in digital safety, and helps vulnerable groups participate safely online.

Real Impact

People lose money, identities, and trust. Awareness reduces harm.

Simple Steps

Small actions — like enabling 2FA — significantly reduce risk.

Community Power

Train one person, protect many. Share what you learn.

Top 3 Ways to Stay Safe Online

Verify Before You Click

Hover links, check sender addresses, and never enter credentials through unknown links.

Check Email Authenticity

Watch for misspellings, odd domains, and urgent demands for money or info.

Enable 2FA

Use two-factor authentication for email, social, and banking accounts.
